The ferry alternative to Senja

An optional scenic swap for Drive Day 2 (Sep 2). Instead of the long inland highway, drive up Vesterålen to Andenes, cross the open sea to Gryllefjord, and reach Senja from its dramatic west coast. It's in season for us — the route runs May 13–Sep 27, 2026. The all-road plan above still stands if you'd rather skip it.

🎟️ Booking & how to pay

There's no reservation — you queue your car at the quay and pay onboard by card. To save 50% on the car fare, register an AutoPASS for ferry card in advance and add the rental's licence plate in your name for the trip (or ask Sixt how ferries are billed).

Getting a spot (early September): In July–August cars queue for hours, sometimes overnight, and the boat fills. Early September is far quieter, so a place is usually easy — but 17:00 is the last sailing of the day, so aim to be in the queue ~75–90 min early (≈15:30). Miss it and the next crossing isn't until 08:45 the following morning.
