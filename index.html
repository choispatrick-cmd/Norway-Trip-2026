01 · BASE

Reine / Hamnøy

Eliassen Rorbuer, Hamnøy · Aug 27–30
+
Reinebringen↗ Moderate
~3 kmDistance RT
~450 mElevation
2–2.5hHike time
~5 minDrive

Lofoten's most iconic view. 1,978 Sherpa-built stone steps straight up to a panorama over Reine, Sakrisøy, Hamnøy and the fjords. Short but relentlessly steep.

🚗 From Eliassen Rorbuer to the Ramsvik Tunnel / Steffenakken trailhead, just outside Reine.
Ryten & Kvalvika Beach↗ Easy–Mod
7–9 kmDistance RT
520–680 mElevation
3–5hHike time
~20–25 minDrive

Gentler climb than Reinebringen, ending at a mini-Trolltunga overhang above golden Kvalvika Beach. Can be extended down to the beach itself.

🚗 To Innersand/Indresand parking, near Fredvang.
Tindstinden↗ Moderate
~6 kmDistance RT
~450 mElevation
3–3.5hHike time
~10 minDrive

A quieter, steep and scrambly hike with views over the village of Å, Lake Ågvatnet, and the open Arctic Ocean. Far fewer crowds than the big names.

🚗 To Sørvågen, parking between Sørvågvatnet & Tindsvatnet lakes.
Munken (North Peak)↗ Hard
~12 kmDistance RT
~910 mElevation
7–8hHike time
~10 minDrive

Full-day trek past scenic lakes and the Munkebu DNT hut to Munken's North Top (the true south summit is climbers-only). Chained rock sections, 360° views of Reinefjord.

🚗 To Sørvågen trailhead — same start as Tindstinden.
Added recommendation
Bunes Beach↗ Easy
~6 kmDistance RT
~100 mElevation
2h + ferryHike time
~5 min + ferryDrive

One of Norway's most beautiful hidden beaches — a short passenger ferry from Reine to Vindstad, then an easy, family-friendly walk over a low pass to pristine white sand ringed by cliffs.

⛴️ ~5 min drive to Reine harbor, then the Reinefjorden ferry to Vindstad (book ahead — only 2–4 sailings/day).
Added recommendation — optional big day
Hermannsdalstinden↗ Hard
~20 kmDistance RT
~1,000 mElevation
8–10hHike time
~10 minDrive

The highest peak on Moskenesøya (1,029 m). A serious full mountain day, often combined with Munkebu hut. Only worth it if you want one truly epic summit push.

🚗 Same Sørvågen trailhead as Munken/Tindstinden.
02 · BASE

Kabelvåg

Nyvågar Rorbuhotell, Storvågvn 22 · Aug 30–Sep 2
+
Includes Henningsvær (~16–20 min drive) and Svolvær (~10 min drive) — both easy day-trip distance.
Festvågtind↗ Moderate
3–5 kmDistance RT
~540 mElevation
2hHike time
~20 minDrive

Steep, rocky climb just outside Henningsvær with a 360° reward — the fishing village spread over its islets, and on clear days a view all the way to the mainland.

🚗 To the Festvågtind roadside trailhead, ~10 min north of Henningsvær.
Presten (Torsketunga)↗ Moderate
~5 kmDistance RT
~500 mElevation
3–4hHike time
~20 minDrive

A quiet extension of the Festvågtind trail to a dramatic slab of rock jutting out with a sheer drop — one of Lofoten's best hidden-gem photo spots, barely any crowds.

🚗 Same Festvågtind trailhead near Henningsvær — the path continues further.
Fløyfjellet & Djevelporten↗ Moderate
4–5.5 kmDistance RT
590–620 mElevation
2.5–3.5hHike time
~10 minDrive

Svolvær's local mountain via a Sherpa staircase, passing the famous Djevelporten (Devil's Gate) — a rock wedged between two cliffs you can stand on — before Fløya's summit.

🚗 Trailhead behind Svolvær Barnehage (kindergarten), central Svolvær.
Added recommendation
Blåtinden & Tuva↗ Hard
~12 kmDistance RT
625 m / 477 mPeak heights
~8hHike time
~10 minDrive

The full "3 peaks" extension of Fløya/Djevelporten — tack on two more summits for a proper full-day ridge adventure over Svolvær. Only for a day you want to go big.

🚗 Same trailhead as Fløya/Djevelporten in Svolvær.
Added recommendation
Matmora↗ Hard
10–12 kmDistance RT
~790–840 mElevation
~5hHike time
~40–45 minDrive

One of Lofoten's most spectacular, least-crowded ridge hikes — a long alpine traverse near Laukvik with jaw-dropping views over Grunnførfjorden. Worth it if you want a wilder, quieter mountain day.

🚗 Via Svolvær, then E10 + Fv888 toward Laukvik/Delp.
03 · BASE

Skaland, Senja

Airbnb (host Ivar), Bergsfjordveien, Skaland · Sep 2–5
+
Husfjellet↗ Moderate
8 kmDistance RT
630–670 mElevation
3–4hHike time
~5–10 minDrive

Senja's most rewarding "easy" big hike — a gradual, family-friendly climb with 360° views over Steinfjorden, Bergsfjorden, and the jagged Okshornan ridge. Almost at your door.

🚗 Trailhead is right in Skaland village center, opposite the supermarket.
Hesten↗ Moderate
5–5.5 kmDistance RT
490–545 mElevation
3–3.5hHike time
~35–40 minDrive

The place to photograph Segla's dramatic north face — most of the iconic Segla shots online are taken from right here. Exposed ridge walk near the top.

🚗 To Fjordgård (paid lot near the Montessori school).
Added recommendation — don't skip this one
Segla↗ Mod–Hard
~5 kmDistance RT
610–640 mElevation
3–4hHike time
~35–40 minDrive

Senja's single most iconic peak — the "sail" that appears on every postcard. Steep, exposed final push (~45° incline) but non-technical. Shares the exact same trailhead as Hesten and Barden, so it costs nothing extra in driving to add.

🚗 Same Fjordgård trailhead as Hesten/Barden.
Barden↗ Moderate
7.5–8 kmDistance RT
570–636 mElevation
3–3.5hHike time
~35–40 minDrive

A quieter alternative to Segla/Hesten with a full 360° panorama over Mefjorden and Ørnfjorden, plus views of Segla, Keipen, and Breitinden. Same trailhead, far fewer people.

🚗 Same Fjordgård trailhead as Segla/Hesten.
Added recommendation
Knuten↗ Easy
~1–2 kmDistance RT
MinimalElevation
15–30 minHike time
~30 minDrive

A rocky hilltop right above Mefjordvær village. Open fjord views on three sides with almost no effort — great for a lazy evening or midnight-sun walk after a bigger hike that day.

🚗 To Mefjordvær village; trail starts a short walk from the harbor.
