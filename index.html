A short, steep, airy climb rising straight from the sea above Henningsvær to a huge view over the village and Vestfjord. Loose rock — proper boots essential. Can be combined with Presten (Torsketunga) from the same trailhead.

Mod–Hard
The summit of Senja's iconic "sail" peak (636 m) with a dizzying view down to Fjordgård. Loose, steep, muddy — a strenuous scramble up top. Can be combined with Barden in a loop from Fjordgård.

Moderate
A rocky ridge with a 360° panorama of Mefjord, Ørnfjord and the Segla/Keipen peaks. Wet, muddy lower sections. Can be combined with Segla in a loop from the same Fjordgård trailhead.
